modelparameters.sympy.logic.utilities.dimacs.load(s)[source]¶

Loads a boolean expression from a string.

Examples

>>> from .dimacs import load
>>> load('1')
cnf_1
>>> load('1 2')
cnf_1 | cnf_2
>>> load('1 \n 2')
cnf_1 & cnf_2
>>> load('1 2 \n 3')
cnf_3 & (cnf_1 | cnf_2)
